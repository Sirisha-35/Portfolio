  • OVER-SPEED E-MAIL ALERT

    • Underwent 45 days Virtual internship programme on the topic “SALESFORCE” organised by BODHA SOFTWARE TECHNOLOGIES .

  • Software Used:

    Salesforce

  • Description:

    Over-Speed E-mail Alert is designed to send an email alert when a person drives a vehicle with overspeed. It sends email alert to the police for the purpose of issuing challan, to relatives and to the owner of the vehicle. This project helps to identify the persons who are driving at overspeed and action will be taken by the police so that they may not repeat the same for the next time.
